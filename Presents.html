Our Story

Built for People Who Live for Moments

Presents started in 2018 when our founders kept asking the same question: why is finding truly great events still so hard? We set out to fix that — and haven't stopped since.

The Journey

From a Weekend Project to a Global Platform

It began in a tiny San Francisco apartment. Our co-founders Sophia and Marcus were frustrated that every event tool felt like a classified-ads site from 2005. So they built the platform they wished existed.

Eight years later, Presents connects tens of thousands of curious, passionate people with the events that define their years — tech summits, jazz nights, culinary adventures, and everything in between.

We're a team of 60 people across 8 cities who believe that the right event can change your trajectory.
